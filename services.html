Technical Services for Battery Materials and Electrolyte Development

Winigen Materials supports battery developers from early material selection through formulation screening and pilot-scale validation. Our services combine battery material sourcing, electrolyte formulation guidance, additive screening strategy, solid-state electrolyte evaluation, and application-specific material matching.

Development Workflow

From problem definition to repeatable validation

A practical, iterative process for narrowing materials, formulations, test conditions, and process risks.

01

Identify

Define the cell chemistry, performance target, failure mode, and screening constraints.

02

Design

Select candidate salts, solvents, additives, solid electrolytes, active materials, and baseline controls.

03

Screen

Compare candidates using retention, Coulombic efficiency, EIS, gas/swelling, rate performance, low-temperature behavior, and post-cycling diagnostics where available.

04

Validate

Move promising systems into realistic cell formats, voltage windows, electrolyte loading, pressure conditions, and formation protocols.

05

Scale

Support transition from lab material screening to pilot-scale validation and repeatable sourcing.
