About Winigen Materials

Battery Materials for the Winning Generation

Winigen Materials supplies battery materials and electrochemical components for advanced energy storage research, development, and early-stage production. We support teams working on lithium-ion, lithium-metal, silicon-anode, sodium-ion, potassium-ion, magnesium-ion, and solid-state battery systems.

Our Focus

Material access with practical electrochemical understanding

Our focus is simple: help battery developers access the right materials, compare them properly, and move more efficiently from laboratory screening toward practical cell validation.

Battery development is often slowed by fragmented material sourcing, incomplete product data, and unclear screening strategy. A material may look attractive on paper, but real cell performance depends on purity, moisture content, particle size, formulation compatibility, interphase chemistry, and processing conditions.

Winigen Materials was built to close that gap.

Our Approach

Select materials with the final cell application in mind
