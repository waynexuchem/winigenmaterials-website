FAQ

Common Questions

What are next-generation salts used for?

They are used to explore alternatives to conventional lithium electrolyte systems, including sodium, magnesium, and potassium chemistries.

Do NaPF6 and other salts list water specifications?

Yes. Product pages list water specifications such as <= 20ppm or <= 50ppm where applicable.

Can Winigen support custom electrolyte formulation with these salts?

Yes. RFQ requests can include salt, solvent, additive, and application requirements.
