Lithium-Ion Electrolyte Applications

Lithium hexafluorophosphate (LiPF6) is a lithium electrolyte salt for lithium-ion electrolyte formulation, additive screening, and battery materials research. Winigen Materials lists this material for RFQ-based supply where low moisture, purity, and compatibility with carbonate or specialty solvent systems are key selection criteria.

  • Comparison with LiFSI, LiTFSI, LiBF4, and other lithium salts
