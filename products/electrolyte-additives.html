FAQ

Common Questions

What are electrolyte additives used for?

Electrolyte additives are used to tune interphase formation, gas behavior, impedance, stability, and cycling performance.

Can additives be requested as part of a formulation?

Yes. Winigen can discuss additive packages together with salts and solvents through the RFQ process.

Are additives listed with CAS numbers?

Many additive product pages include CAS numbers and typical physical or grade information.
