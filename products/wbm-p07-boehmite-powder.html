WBM-P07 Fine Boehmite Powder

Fine boehmite powder for ceramic coating slurry preparation and formulation development.

Product Details

Maximum local formulation flexibility

WBM-P07 is a Winigen-designated fine boehmite powder grade for local ceramic-slurry formulation. It is positioned for customers who prefer shipment and storage robustness and local control over binder, solvent, solids, dispersion, and rheology.
